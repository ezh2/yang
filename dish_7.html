Stir Fried Spoon Cabbage with Ginger

Ginger


Health Benefits: Ginger contains several health benefits and boasts powerful medicinal properties. It is best known to reduce nausea, but it also has anti-inflammatory characteristics. Ginger also can help reduce pain from osteoarthritis, reduce blood pressure, and may help prevent cancer.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Spoon cabbage leaves 130g
Shredded ginger 8g
Garlic × 1
Salt ½ teaspoon
Bonito powder ⅓ teaspoon
Olive oil 1 spoon
Water 10 ml

Steps

  1. Cut spoon cabbage into pieces.
  2. Peel off ginger skin and shred the ginger.
  3. Pour olive oil (1 tablespoon) into a hot pan, add garlic over medium heat.
  4. Add ginger and stir for about 1 min.
  5. Add spoon cabbage into the frying pan and stir fry for 2 mins.
  6. Add salt and bonito powder.
  7. Add 10ml water and cook for 1 min.
  8. Pour out all spoon cabbage and ginger, and it’s ready to serve.
