Fried Tomato Eggs

Tomato


Health Benefits: Tomatoes are low in calories and provide important nutrients like vitamin C and potassium. They're also rich in antioxidants—one called lycopene, responsible for tomatoes' characteristic color, is linked to several benefits, such as a reduced risk of heart disease and certain cancers.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Tomatoes - 2 ripe ones
Egg - 2 big ones
Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
Chopped Green onion - 1g
Salt - ½ teaspoon
Bonito powder - ½ teaspoon
Water - 700 ml
Brown sugar - 2g

Steps

  1. Boil 650ml water.
  2. When the water is boiling, put 2 tomatoes into the hot water and soak for 3 mins till the skin becomes soft.
  3. Take out the tomatoes from the water and peel off their skin gently.
  4. Whisk 2 eggs into a bowl.
  5. Chop 2 tomatoes into cube size.
  6. Put olive oil into a wok, add tomatoes and fry for 3 mins until it becomes paste.
  7. Add eggs into the wok along with the tomatoes and fry till the eggs turn brown.
  8. Add salt, brown sugar, and bonito powder with low heat.
  9. Turn off the heat and dress with some green onion on the top.
