Lotus Root Soup

Lotus Root


Health Benefits: Eating with the peels can effectively absorb potassium to reduce edema, vitamin B1 to eliminate fatigue, calcium to calm nerves, and dietary fiber to prevent constipation

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Lotus root - 100g
Vegetable soup base - 400ml
Beech Mushroom - peel into smaller pieces, 50g
Tomato - cut into one-bite servings
Tender tofu - cut into 1.5cm pieces - 100g
Soy sauce - 2 tablespoons
Sugar - â…– tablespoon
Chopped shallots - x3
Olive oil - adequate amount

Steps

  1. Grind the lotus root with the skin into mud using a mud grinder.
  2. Put the vegetable stock and beech mushrooms in a pot, heat over medium heat, bring to a boil, and add tofu, tomato, soy sauce, and sugar, seasoned appropriately.
  3. Add the lotus root puree from step 1 into step 2, stir well, and wait for the lotus root to soften.
  4. Remove the mixture from the heat, put it into a container, and sprinkle with chopped shallots at the end.
