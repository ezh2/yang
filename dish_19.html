Okra Salad with Japanese Sesame Sauce

Okra


Health Benefits: Okra is rich in vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants that help reduce the risk of serious health conditions like cancer, diabetes, stroke, and heart disease. Okra is also a good source of: Magnesium.

Ingredients (for 1 servings)

Water 600 ml
Olive oil ½ teaspoon
Ice cubes × 15
Okra 100 g
Japanese sesame sauce 30 ml

Steps

  1. Cut the head of the okra and clean under running water.
  2. Boil 600ml water and put in all okra; cook for 4 mins.
  3. Add a little bit of olive oil into the water (to keep its vivid color).
  4. Take out okra from hot water and put it into a bowl with cold water and ice cubes for 1 min.
  5. Take out okra from the chilly water and dry it. Put them on a plate.
  6. Topped with Japanese sesame sauce and it's ready to serve.
