Miso Eggplant

Eggplant


Health Benefits: Eggplant is rich in vitamins and minerals that support immunity, skin health and digestive health. Despite these potential benefits, consuming too much eggplant can lead to some adverse effects on your body. These include: Digestive issues such as gas and bloating.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Eggplant × 1, cut into 5cm long
Salt × 1 teaspoon
Ginger - 5g, sliced
Chopped green onion × 3g
Japanese miso sauce (light yellow color) × 10g
Olive oil × 2 teaspoons
Water 30ml
White wine 15ml
Sweet sake 10ml

Steps

  1. Put 1 teaspoon of salt on the dry and cleaned eggplant, and massage them for 1 min to remove excess water.
  2. Add 2 teaspoons of olive oil to a wok along with sliced ginger and cook for 1 min.
  3. Put Japanese Miso sauce in a small bowl and dissolve it with white wine.
  4. Put the eggplant and the liquid Japanese Miso sauce in the wok and fry for 3 mins until it turns soft and is mixed with the miso sauce.
  5. Add sweet sake (10ml) to the wok and fry for another 30 seconds, then serve on a plate.
