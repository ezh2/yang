Fried Cabbage with Beef

Cabbage


Health Benefits: Cabbage is highly nutritious and rich in vitamin C, fiber, and vitamin K. What’s more, some research suggests that it may support digestion, improve heart health, and decrease inflammation.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Cabbage - 180g, cut into bite-sized pieces
Beef - 130g, thinly sliced
Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
Soy sauce - 3 (40ml each)
Bonito - 1 teaspoon
Red chili - 2 pieces

Steps

  1. Put olive oil into a wok and add all beef. Fry with medium heat for 2 minutes. Only cook to medium rare and pour out.
  2. Put olive oil and fry with all cabbage leaves until they become soft and small.
  3. Add soy sauce and bonito into the wok along with the beef and cabbage.
  4. Dress with 2 pieces of red chili.
