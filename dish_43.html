Pumpkin Pudding

Pumpkin


Health Benefits: In addition to beta carotene, pumpkins offer vitamin C, vitamin E, iron, and folate -- all of which strengthen your immune system. More pumpkin in your diet can help your immune cells work better to ward off germs and speed healing when you get a wound.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Pumpkin × 180g, cubed
Milk - 300ml
Sugar - 60g
Butter - 50g
Pancake powder - 50g
Egg × 2

Steps

  1. Peel off pumpkin’s skin and cut it into 4×4 cm pieces.
  2. Put the pumpkin in a steamer.
  3. Steam for 20 minutes until it's soft.
  4. Put all ingredients in the blender and mix them well.
  5. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees.
  6. Pour out the pumpkin liquid from the blender and pour all the liquid into a pound cake mold.
  7. Bake for 70 minutes at 190 degrees.
  8. When the center of the pumpkin pudding is fully baked, it’s ready to serve in a small bowl.
