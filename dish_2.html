Bamboo Salad

Bamboo


Health Benefits: With so many vitamins and minerals packed into these sturdy shoots, it comes as no surprise that there are a number of health benefits to bamboo. It may lower cholesterol and stimulate appetite

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

â€‹Fresh bamboo 190g
Mayonnaise 30g
Water 3000 ml

Steps

  1. Clean the bamboo under running water.
  2. Cut a small slit with a knife on the bottom of the bamboo.
  3. Boil 1500 ml of water.
  4. After the water is boiled, put the bamboo into the water and cook for 20 mins.
  5. Pour out all the hot water and wait for 20 mins until the bamboo is cold.
  6. Pour fresh cold water (1500ml) and soak the boiled bamboo.
  7. Put the bamboo with cold water in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  8. Take out the bamboo from the cold water, take off the hard skin of the bamboo, and chop the bamboo shoots into chunks.
  9. Serve with 30g of Mayonnaise on the side.
