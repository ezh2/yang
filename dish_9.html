Loofah & Clam Soup

Loofah


Health Benefits: Reduce arthritis, Treat Anemia, Skin health, Prevent diabetes, Brain function

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Loofah×1
clam×250g
Bonito powder ½ teaspoon
Shredded ginger 2g
garlic×1​
Olive oil 2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Soak the clams in 3% saltwater for 1 to 2 hours to spit out sand, then change the water 2 to 3 times and wash them thoroughly.
  2. Peel off loofah’s skin and slice it about 1 inch wide.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil into the wok, then add garlic, ginger, and loofah. Stir fry until the loofah becomes half soft.
  4. Add all clams into the wok, cover with a pan lid, and heat until the clams are all opened (about 6 minutes).
  5. After the clams are all opened, turn off the heat, add salt and bonito powder, and serve on a plate.
