Sweet Potato Pancake

Sweet Potato


Health Benefits: Sweet potatoes are nutritious, packing a good amount of vitamin A, vitamin C, and manganese into each serving. They also have anticancer properties and may promote immune function and other health benefits.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Sweet potato - 160g
Water 400ml
Corn × 60g
Carrot - sliced × 60g
Egg × 1
Green onion × 1, about 30g
Salt × 1 teaspoon
Bonito powder × ½ teaspoon
All-purpose flour × 100g
Sunflower seed oil × 300ml

Steps

  1. Peel off the skin of sweet potato and cut it into thin strips.
  2. Take a clean bowl, put all ingredients inside, and mix them.
  3. Add 400ml of water along with all-purpose flour in the bowl as well. Mix it until all the flour is fully dissolved in the water.
  4. Heat up a frying pan with 300ml of oil and wait until it reaches 170 degrees Celsius.
  5. Put 2 spoon-sized portions of sweet potato mix in the oil and fry for 5 mins.
  6. When it becomes golden at the edges, take out the sweet potato pancakes and place them on a kitchen towel to remove excess oil. They are ready to serve.
