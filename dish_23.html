Potato, Veggie and Oxtail Soup

Potato


Health Benefits: They're rich in vitamin C, which is an antioxidant. Potatoes were a life-saving food source in early times because the vitamin C prevented scurvy. Another major nutrient in potatoes is potassium, an electrolyte which aids in the workings of our heart, muscles, and nervous system.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Potato×1, cut into pieces
Tomato×1, cut into pieces
Onion×1, cut into pieces
Salt×2 teaspoon
Water×1200 ml
Oxtail×200g

Steps

  1. Wash potato, tomato.
  2. Cut potato, tomato, and onion into pieces. Wait aside.
  3. Boil 500ml hot water, put oxtail into hot water and boil for 5 mins, take oxtail from hot water and wash it with clean water.
  4. Boil 700ml water and put all veggie and oxtail in the soup.
  5. Cook for 1.5 hours until all veggie become soft.
  6. The soup will become unclear, add salt in the soup and it's ready to serve in a bowl.
