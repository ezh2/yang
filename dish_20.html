Okra Salad with Tofu Salad

Okra


Health Benefits: Okra is rich in vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants that help reduce the risk of serious health conditions like cancer, diabetes, stroke, and heart disease. Okra is also a good source of: Magnesium.

Ingredients (for 3 servings)

Water 300 ml
Olive oil ½ teaspoon
Tofu × 1 (200g)
Okra 40 g
Bonito flakes × 10g
Sesame oil × 30ml
Japanese sweet soy sauce × 2 spoon
Thick soy sauce × 2 spoon

Steps

  1. Cut the head of the okra and clean under running water.
  2. Boil 300ml water and put in all okra; cook for 4 mins.
  3. Add a little bit of olive oil into the water (to keep its vivid color).
  4. Take out okra from hot water and cut into 0.5cm pieces.
  5. Place clean tofu on a kitchen towel and dry it for 10 min, until excess water comes out from the tofu.
  6. Put sliced okra on tofu and add sesame oil, Japanese sweet soy sauce, and thick soy sauce on top of the tofu salad.
  7. Dress with some bonito flakes on top, and it's ready to serve.
