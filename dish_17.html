Eggplant Salad

Eggplant


Health Benefits: Eggplant is rich in vitamins and minerals that support immunity, skin health and digestive health. Despite these potential benefits, consuming too much eggplant can lead to some adverse effects on your body. These include: Digestive issues such as gas and bloating.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Eggplant × 1, cut into 5cm
Chopped garlic × 2
Chopped red pepper × 3g
Chopped ginger × 3g
Sweet soy sauce × 10ml
Black vinegar × 10ml
Sesame oil × 15ml
Chopped green onion × 3g
Olive oil × 2 teaspoons

Steps

  1. Boil 600ml water.
  2. Add eggplant into boiling water and cover with a lid on the top in order to keep the skin color of the eggplant.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes and pour out all eggplant and dry off excess water.
  4. Put chopped garlic, red pepper, and ginger along with olive oil into a hot pan.
  5. Put sweet soy sauce into the pan and mix it well. This is the sauce for the eggplant salad.
  6. Put the eggplant on a clean white plate and top it with the salad sauce.
  7. Dress some green onion and sesame oil on the top.
