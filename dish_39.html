Lettuce Salad

Lettuce leaves


Health Benefits: Lettuce is an excellent source of beta carotene (vitamin A) which is needed for healthy skin, bones, and eyes. Lettuce is a fair source of folate, which is needed for healthy cells and the healthy growth of babies during pregnancy in order to prevent neural tube defects.

Ingredients (for 1 servings)

Mix lettuce leaves - 100g
Tomato × 50g, cut into pieces
Avocado × 50g
Cucumber × 30g
Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
Salt × 1g
Pepper × 1g

Steps

  1. Put all green lettuce, tomato, avocado, and cucumber in a salad bowl.
  2. Dress it with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. It's ready to serve.
