Roasted Broccoli Skewers with Herb Bread Flour

Broccoli


Health Benefits: Broccoli stems contain the antioxidant dendrobetin, which improves blood circulation

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Broccoli-180g
Chicken breast-160g
Cream cheese-80g
Combo A
Mashed garlic-1 slice
Cheese powder-2 tablespoon
Breadcrumbs-2 tablespoon
Olive oil-2 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Divide the broccoli into small florets (12 florets) and place them in a heat-resistant container. Cover with plastic wrap and heat in a microwave oven at 500 watts for about 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  2. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces (8 equal pieces). Repeat the following steps 2 times in the order of small broccoli, broccoli stem, chicken, skewer with bamboo skewers, and finally skewer with small broccoli. You should have 4 strings in total.
  3. Spread cream cheese on top of the skewers and sprinkle mixture A on top of the cream cheese.
  4. Lay a layer of aluminum foil on a baking tray and arrange the skewers on it. Be sure to wrap the exposed skewers with aluminum foil to prevent them from burning. Put the whole pan into the oven (800~1000 watt oven) and bake for about 20 minutes.
  5. When the meat is fully cooked, serve it on a plate.
