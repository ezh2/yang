Bamboo Shoot with Pork and Fresh Black Fungus

Bamboo


Health Benefits: With so many vitamins and minerals packed into these sturdy shoots, it comes as no surprise that there are a number of health benefits to bamboo. It may lower cholesterol and stimulates appetite

Ingredients (for 1 servings)

Shredded pork - 38g
Bamboo shoot - 120g
Shredded black fungus - 25g
Salt - 1 teaspoon
Bonito powder - â…“ Teaspoon
Olive oil - 2 teaspoon
Water - 600 ml

Steps

  1. Boil 600 ml water.
  2. Put bamboo shoot into hot water and cook for 10 mins.
  3. Take out bamboo shoot from water and cut into stripes.
  4. Put olive oil into a wok and add a garlic till it turns brown color.
  5. Add shredded pork in the wok with medium heat.
  6. Add bamboo shoot and black fungus and fry for 3-4 mins.
  7. Add salt and bonito before turning off the heat.
  8. Ready to serve.
