Fried Bean Sprouts

Bean Sprouts


Health Benefits: Bean sprouts have a high protein content, with an average of five grams of protein per cup. Mung beans are high in vitamin C and vitamin B6, as well as iron, riboflavin, pantothenic acid, niacin, thiamine, zinc, calcium, phosphorus, magnesium, potassium, and copper. Bean sprouts are also incredibly low in fat, cholesterol-free, and saturated fat-free. The amount of dietary fiber is high (more than 2.5 grams per cup), and there are a few additional complex carbs.

Ingredients (for 1 servings)

Bean sprouts - 180g
Sliced chili × 2g
Green onion × 50g, cut into 5 cm long
Garlic × 1
Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
Salt × ½ teaspoon
Bonito powder × ½ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Wash bean sprouts and dry them with a kitchen towel.
  2. Turn on the heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a wok. When it’s getting hot, put one garlic until it turns golden color at the edge.
  3. Put sliced chili, green onion, and bean sprouts in the wok, stir-fry for 3 mins.
  4. Add salt and bonito powder in the wok, and it's ready to serve on a plate.
