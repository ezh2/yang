Sweet Potato Fries

Sweet Potato


Health Benefits: Sweet potatoes are nutritious, packing a good amount of vitamin A, vitamin C, and manganese into each serving. They also have anticancer properties and may promote immune function and other health benefits.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Sweet potato - 450g
Sunflower seed oil Ã— 300ml
Salt - 2g
Pepper - 2g

Steps

  1. Peel off the skin of the sweet potato, cut it into thin strips.
  2. Soak all sweet potato strips in clean water for 20 minutes.
  3. Pour out the water and dry the sweet potato strips with a kitchen towel.
  4. Take a clean oven pan and put oven paper on it.
  5. Preheat the oven to 185 degrees.
  6. Put all sweet potato strips on the oven plate and pour 30 ml of oil, salt, and pepper mixed with the sweet potatoes.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes at 185 degrees. When the sweet potatoes become a golden brown color, they're ready to take out from the oven.
  8. Serve in a small bowl.
