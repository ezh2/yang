Bamboo Shoot with Rib Soup

Bamboo


Health Benefits: With so many vitamins and minerals packed into these sturdy shoots, it comes as no surprise that there are a number of health benefits to bamboo. It may lower cholesterol and stimulate appetite

Ingredients (for 3 servings)

Water 3300 ml
Chopped pork rib - 320g
Bamboo shoot 200g
Shredded Ginger 70g
Salt ½ teaspoon
Bonito powder ⅓ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Boil 1500 ml water.
  2. Put pork rib into hot water and cook for 5 minutes until white bubbles come out on the surface of the hot water, then pour out all dirty water.
  3. Clean and wash the pork rib with clean water.
  4. Boil 1800 ml water, and put all shredded ginger into the water.
  5. Add pork rib into the soup with medium heat for 3 minutes.
  6. Add bamboo shoot into the soup and cook for around 1.5 hours with low heat.
  7. Add salt and bonito before turning off the heat.
  8. Ready to serve.
