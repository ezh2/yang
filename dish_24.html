Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin


Health Benefits: In addition to beta carotene, pumpkins offer vitamin C, vitamin E, iron, and folate -- all of which strengthen your immune system. More pumpkin in your diet can help your immune cells work better to ward off germs and speed healing when you get a wound.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Pumpkin×400g
Chicken broth×500ml
Potato×100g
Water×100ml
Salt×1 spoon
Black pepper×3g

Steps

  1. Peel off pumpkin’s skin and potato skin and cut into pieces.
  2. Put pumpkin and potatoes in a steamer.
  3. Steam for 25 minutes until they are soft.
  4. Put the steamed pumpkin and potatoes in a blender, add 100 ml of water and blend it well.
  5. Pour out all the liquid from the blender into a soup bowl and add 500 ml of chicken broth to the bowl, cook it for 20 minutes.
  6. Add salt and pepper to the bowl, and it’s ready to serve.
