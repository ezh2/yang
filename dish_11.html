Garlic Cucumber Salad

Cucumber


Health Benefits: Cucumbers are a good source of potassium, magnesium and dietary fibre. These nutrients are known to lower blood pressure, thus reducing the risk of heart diseases. Research has also proved that regular consumption of cucumber juice was helpful in reducing blood pressure, in elderly people with hypertension.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

Cucumber × 3 (about 300g)
Garlic × 3
Chili × 5g
Salt × 2 teaspoon
Brown sugar × 3 teaspoon
Vinegar × 50ml
Sesame oil × 20ml

Steps

  1. Wash cucumber under running water and dry it with a kitchen towel.
  2. Smash cucumbers into pieces with a meat pounder.
  3. Put cucumbers along with salt in a bowl, wait 15 minutes, and then pour out the excess water from under the bowl.
  4. Add all ingredients into the bowl and wait for 20 minutes.
