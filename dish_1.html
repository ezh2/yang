Sweet Taro

Taro


Health Benefits: The sticky part between the taro’s peel is galactan. It can reduce blood sugar levels and blood cholesterol, which can effectively prevent diseases caused by bad living habits.

Ingredients (for 5 servings)

Taro -1 big one, about 600g, cut into cube size.
Rock sugar × 200g
White wine × 20ml
Water × 600ml​

Steps

  1. Clean the skin of taro and peel off its skin.
  2. Cut taro into big cube size and soak them in clean water for 10 min.
  3. When the water become unclear, wash out taro under running water.
  4. Take a clean bowl, put taro and fill it with 600ml water.
  5. Put the bowl in the steamer, and cook for about 40 mins until the toro is fully soften by the steamer.
  6. Add 200g of rock sugar and 20ml of white wine along with taro in the bowl. Keep the bowl in the steamer for another 15 mins until the sugar is totally solved.
  7. After 15 mins, take out the bowl from steamer and wait for cool down.
  8. Keep sweet taro in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours.
  9. Serve on a plate.
